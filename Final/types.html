Chips

Chips, also known as crisps in some countries, are thin and crispy slices of potatoes or other starchy ingredients. They are a popular type of snack enjoyed around the world.



Popcorn

Popcorn is a type of corn kernel that puffs up and expands when heated. It is a popular snack enjoyed by people of all ages, especially during movie nights, sporting events, or as a general snack option.



Nuts

Nuts are edible seeds enclosed in a hard shell that are derived from various plants. They are a popular snack option and can also be used in cooking, baking, and as ingredients in a variety of dishes.



Cookie

A cookie is a type of baked sweet treat that is typically small, flat, and round in shape. Cookies are enjoyed worldwide and come in a wide variety of flavors, textures, and types.



Chocolate

Chocolate is a food product derived from the beans of the cacao tree (Theobroma cacao). It is one of the most popular and widely consumed flavors in the world. Chocolate is known for its rich, sweet taste and creamy texture, and it is used in a wide range of confections, desserts, and beverages.


Gum

Gum, short for chewing gum, is a type of soft, flavored confectionery that is designed to be chewed but not swallowed. It is typically made from a combination of gum base, sweeteners, flavorings, and softeners. Gum comes in various shapes, sizes, and flavors, offering a range of options for consumers.


Sugary drinks

Sugary drinks, also known as sugar-sweetened beverages or soft drinks, are beverages that contain added sugars. These drinks are typically sweetened with various forms of sugar, such as sucrose (table sugar), high fructose corn syrup, or other sweeteners.


Granola Bars

Granola bars are a type of snack bar made from a mixture of rolled oats, nuts, seeds, dried fruits, sweeteners, and other ingredients. They are often pressed together into a bar shape and baked or refrigerated until firm. Granola bars are known for their convenience.
